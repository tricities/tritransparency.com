Video meetings ✱ ⭐️ ✱ ✱

Meetings should be recorded, streamed, with a searchable archive available online in their entirety. As many public meetings as possible should be available in this way, using the same system across the board.

Kennewick just started streaming, so there is limited archival footage.

Pasco gets the best-in-class score for having failt just City Council meetings.

Richland does not have either the council’s workshops or their regular pre-meeting workshop online.

West Richland meetings are aired live on Public Access channel 191, but not available online at all.
